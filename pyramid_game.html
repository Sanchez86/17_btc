Fair game

As each round begins the service will generate a random number from 0 to 1 (for example, 0.21492) + salt. Salt is a random set of symbols (for example: iGwpMtsYs). These symbols are used to prevent the round's number decryption. The round's number and salt are encrypted by the service through algorithm sha224 and are shown as each round begins.

In the end of the round previously encrypted number is multiplied by the total bank of the round points, thus getting the number of the winning point.

Pls see the example: In the end the total round bank is 83657 points, and the random number was 0.21492. So the service multiplies this number by 83657 points and gets the number 17979, that’s will be the winning point. The winner of the round will be the player, who deposited this point.

The fair play stands at the principle that we cannot know what exactly the points bank will be in the end of the round, and the random number for multiplication is given right in the beginning of the game. That's why you can be sure that we have no ability to influence the game results .

You can check that the result of the game wasn’t prearranged. For this, use any of the online services, for example, http://www.miniwebtool.com/sha224-hash-generator/. In the end of the round take the round's number+salt, which was encrypted, and repeatedly encrypt it at the service. You will see the same hash value as the one specified in the beginning of the round. This will prove the accuracy of determining the winning point.

How I can play Roulette?

The players deposit certain amount of points into the game. After each spin there is only one winner who gets all the points. The spin lasts 60 seconds. The more points you deposit, the higher winning chances you have. But there is the real chance to win even with the smallest bet!

The winner is determined by our fair play system. Read more in the “Fair Game” tab.
